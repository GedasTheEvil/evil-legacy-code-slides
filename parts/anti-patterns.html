The most common ani-patterns

The almighty method/function

How and why people can write 500+ lines-long method is hard to understand. Some are thousands of lines long. Problems created:

  • Hard to understand
  • Untestable
  • Usually cannot be reused
  • Obviously try to do more than they should
  • Take a huge amount of time to fix/refactor

Copy/Paste/Mutate

Instead of separating code into reusable classes or components, code was copied and pasted.

Over the time the copy-pasted parts have been changed differently, and became different mutations of the same code.
